Divergent Doodles

Stickers and Stationery for Mental Health

I started therapy for executive disfuntion 2 years ago for my sever ADHD. When I started I looked for cute planners, stickers, and other organizational supplies to help aid in the process of overcoming my struggles. To my dismay, and later delight, I found a gap in the market. It was at that moment I decided to start "Divergent Doodles".

I had always wanted to do something that allowed me to help other, and I was blessed to have a chance to do just that while following my passion for art. I look forward to putting a smile one your face and hope in your heart while learning to overcome your own struggles.

Beehive Sleep Tracker

Getting a constistant and healthy amount of sleep is very important for your mental health. This beehive helps you by using lanterns to decorate the hive. Too much sleep is a large lantern that covers up all the light. Too little sleep is a tiny lantern that is very dark to show less energy. The right amount of sleep is a perfectly sized lantern that has the right amount of glow.
